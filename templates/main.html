guarantee CR counter:
only compute target 5* probabilities for 5*s
usage
basic use: tell me your pity and number of wishes, i calculate the probability of your next 5* and run a simulation

to get 5* probs for all numbers of wishes, set wishes=0

assumptions
5* char probs: 0.6% at pity<74, +6% for each pity after 73
target char probs: 50% on non-guaranteed 5*; CR prob=0% for CR<2, 50% for CR=2, 100% otherwise
5* weapon probs: 0.7% at pity<63, +7% for each pity after 62
target weapon probs: 37.5% on non-guaranteed 5*; no CR
